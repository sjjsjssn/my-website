Title:

THE EFFECTIVENESS OF THE DROP-OFF AND PICK-UP SYSTEM AND ITS IMPACT ON THE ENVIRONMENT OF THE STUDENTS AT FOUNDATION PREPARATORY ACADEMY: A QUANTITATIVE STUDY

Rationale:

Foundation Preparatory Academy introduced a designated drop-off and pick-up zone to ease rush hour traffic and provide convenience for students and parents. However, the enclosed design has led to unintended issues such as poor air quality, noise pollution, and safety concerns. If these problems continue, drivers may stop using the area, worsening congestion elsewhere.
