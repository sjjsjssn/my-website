Research Methodology

In order to assess the efficiency of the drop-off and pick-up location as well as its environmental impact, particularly with regard to carbon monoxide emissions, our study employed a quantitative, descriptive, and correlational research design. We used Google Forms to collect information from 24 parents and 103 students about air pollution awareness, idling behaviour, and traffic flow. The findings gave us a better understanding of how the current system impacts daily activities and the environment.
