Statement of the Problem & Significance of the Study

Statement of the Problem

This study examines the effectiveness and environmental impact of the pick-up and drop-off system at Foundation Preparatory Academy. It focuses on how vehicle idling, traffic congestion, and other behaviors within the designated zones affect air quality and contribute to pollution. By gathering data from parents and students through surveys, the research aims to identify key issues in the current system and explore practical, eco-friendly solutions to improve safety, efficiency, and environmental sustainability on campus.
